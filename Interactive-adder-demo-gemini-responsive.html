Interactive 1-bit Full Adder

Adding two 1-bit numbers is the fundamental operation in any digital computer. Including "Carry_in" and "Carry_out" bits allow adders to be cascaded to make N-bit adders. The adder has three binary inputs (A, B, C_in) and two binary outputs (Sum, C_out).

The adder below is the classic 1-bit form made of AND, OR and Exclusive OR gates. There is a 2-relay equivalent form described elsewhere.
